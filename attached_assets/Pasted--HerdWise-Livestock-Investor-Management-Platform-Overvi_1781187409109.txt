# HerdWise – Livestock Investor Management Platform

## Overview

HerdWise is a cloud-based livestock management platform designed for cattle fattening farms that manage cattle on behalf of multiple investors.

The farm owner manages all cattle on the farm, while investors can log in and view only the cattle they own.

The system must replace paper-based livestock records and provide real-time access to cattle information, weight records, treatment history, and inventory reports.

---

# User Roles

## Farm Owner (Administrator)

The farm owner has full access to the system and can:

* View all cattle on the farm.
* View all investors.
* Add, edit, and remove investors.
* Register new cattle.
* Assign cattle to investors.
* Record cattle weights.
* Record vaccinations and treatments.
* Upload cattle photos.
* Update cattle status.
* Generate reports.
* Manage user accounts.

---

## Investor

Investors can:

* Log in securely.
* View only cattle assigned to them.
* View cattle details.
* View weight history.
* View treatment and vaccination history.
* View cattle photos.
* Download reports related to their cattle.

Investors must never be able to see cattle belonging to other investors.

* Total cattle on farm.

* Number of active cattle.
* Number of cattle sold.
* Number of vaccinations due.
* Recently added cattle.

## Investor Dashboard

Display:

* Total cattle owned.

---

# Investor Management

Each investor record must contain:

* Investor ID
* Full Name
* Email Address
* Phone Number
* Date Joined
* Active / Inactive Status

Each investor must have their own login credentials.

---

# Cattle Management

Each animal must have:

## Identification

* Farm Tag Number (Unique)
* Previous Tag Number
* RFID Number (Future Feature)

## Ownership

* Assigned Investor
* Date Received

## Animal Information

* Breed
* Colour
* Description
* Sex
* Estimated Age
* Source Lot

## Status

* Active
* Sold
* Deceased
* Transferred
* Quarantined

---

# Weight Tracking

Each animal must maintain a complete weight history.

Weight records must include:

* Date Recorded
* Weight (kg)
* Notes
* Captured By

The system must display weight gain trends and graphs.

---

# Vaccination and Treatment Records

Each animal must maintain a complete medical history.

Records must include:

* Treatment Date
* Vaccine or Treatment Name
* Batch Number
* Administered By
* Notes
* Next Due Date

Examples:

* 3-in-1 Vaccine
* Deworming
* Dipping
* Antibiotic Treatment

---

# Photo Management

Each animal must support multiple photos.

Examples:

* Front View
* Side View
* Tag Photo
* Health Inspection Photo

Photos must be available to the farm owner and the assigned investor.

---

# Search and Filtering

The system must allow searching by:

* Tag Number
* Investor
* Breed
* Status
* Date Received

The system must allow filtering by:

* Investor
* Breed
* Status
* Vaccination Due
* Weight Range

---

# Reports

Generate reports for:

## Farm Owner

* Complete cattle inventory.
* Cattle by investor.
* Vaccination due report.
* Weight gain report.
* Active vs sold cattle.

## Investor

* My cattle inventory.
* Weight history.
* Treatment history.
* Cattle photos.

---

# Security Requirements

* Secure login system.
* Role-based permissions.
* Investors can only access their own cattle.
* Administrators have full access.
* All data must be securely stored.

---

# Future Features

* RFID integration.
* Mobile application.
* WhatsApp notifications.
* Vaccination reminders.
* Feed management.

# Main Goal

Create a modern digital livestock management system that replaces paper records, gives the farm owner complete visibility of all cattle on the farm, and allows each investor to securely monitor only the cattle they own from anywhere.
